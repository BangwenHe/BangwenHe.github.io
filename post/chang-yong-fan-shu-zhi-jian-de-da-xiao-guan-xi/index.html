2范数与无穷范数：∥x∥∞=max⁡i∣xi∣≤∥x∥2=∑i=1n∣xi∣2≤n∥x∥∞\| x \|_\infty = \max _i |x_i| \le \| x \|_2 = \sqrt {\sum_{i=1}^n |x_i|^2} \le \sqrt{n} \| x \|_\infty∥x∥∞​=maxi​∣xi​∣≤∥x∥2​=∑i=1n​∣xi​∣2​≤n​∥x∥∞​
